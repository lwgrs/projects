**Deep Space 9 with U.S.S. Defiant NX-74205 and Cardassian Keldon-Class Cruiser** plastic model kit by [AMT. Model no. 1245](https://www.greathobbies.com/productinfo/?prod_id=AMT1245). The model is a clear plastic to allow for installing a lighting kit, so required painting to reflect the station's true colours. No lighting kit was installed.
