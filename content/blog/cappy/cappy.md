The local baseball team, the [Ottawa Titans](https://ottawatitans.com), released a fun [Build-A-Cappy Block Set](https://shop.ottawatitans.com/build-a-cappy-block-set.html). It is a slight LEGO knock-off, relying on similar studs but also some interlocking pieces. It was a *little* frustrating to build until I figured out how it worked.
